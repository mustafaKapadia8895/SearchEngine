<p>The <b>Landscape of the Pico Island Vineyard Culture</b> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Unesco_World_Heritage_Site" title="Unesco World Heritage Site">Unesco World Heritage Site</a> on <a href="/wiki/Pico_Island" title="Pico Island">Pico Island</a>.</p>
<p>The vineyard is divided into plots (currais) protected by walls (paredes, murinhos). The walls are built with <a href="/wiki/Basalt" title="Basalt">basalt</a> blocks that have been weathered and broken up and stacked without mortar. <a href="/wiki/Viticulture" title="Viticulture">Viticulture</a> dates back to the 15th century.</p>
