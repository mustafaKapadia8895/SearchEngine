<p><b>Enkapune Ya Muto</b>, also known as <b>Twilight Cave</b>, is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Late_Stone_Age" title="Late Stone Age">Late Stone Age</a> site on the <a href="/wiki/Mau_Escarpment" title="Mau Escarpment">Mau Escarpment</a> of <a href="/wiki/Kenya" title="Kenya">Kenya</a>. Beads made of perforated ostrich egg shells found at the site have been dated to 40,000 years ago. The beads found at the site represent the early human use of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jewelry" title="Jewelry">personal ornaments</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MyUser_Archaeology.about.com_March_7_2017c_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-MyUser_Archaeology.about.com_March_7_2017c-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
