<p>The <b>yellowtail flounder</b> (<i><b>Pleuronectes ferruginea</b></i> or <i><b>Limanda ferruginea</b></i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> of <a href="/wiki/Fish" title="Fish">fish</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Pleuronectidae" title="Pleuronectidae">Pleuronectidae</a> family. It is found in <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a> and the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>.</p>
