<p><b>Horse mackerel</b> is a vague vernacular term for a range of <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> of <a href="/wiki/Fish" title="Fish">fish</a> throughout the English-speaking world. It is commonly applied to <a href="/wiki/Pelagic_fish" title="Pelagic fish">pelagic fishes</a>, especially of the <a href="/wiki/Carangidae" title="Carangidae">Carangidae</a> (jack mackerels and scads) family, most commonly those of the <a href="/wiki/Genus" title="Genus">genera</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Trachurus" title="Trachurus">Trachurus</a></i> or <i><a href="/wiki/Caranx" title="Caranx">Caranx</a></i>. Species known as "horse mackerel" in one English dialect or another include:</p>
<ul>
<li><i>Alectis indicus</i>, <a href="/wiki/Indian_threadfish" title="Indian threadfish">Indian threadfish</a> (Malaysia)</li>
<li><i>Caranx crysos</i>, <a href="/wiki/Blue_runner" title="Blue runner">blue runner</a> (Guadaloupe, Martinique)</li>
<li><i>Caranx hippos</i>, <a href="/wiki/Crevalle_jack" title="Crevalle jack">Crevalle jack</a> (Guyana, India)</li>
<li><i>Megalaspis cordyla</i>, <a href="/wiki/Torpedo_scad" title="Torpedo scad">torpedo scad</a> (India)</li>
<li><i>Naucrates ductor</i>, <a href="/wiki/Pilot_fish" title="Pilot fish">pilot fish</a></li>
<li><i>Sarda australis</i>, <a href="/wiki/Australian_bonito" title="Australian bonito">Australian bonito</a> (Australia)</li>
<li>various <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saurel" title="Saurel">saurel</a> of the Pacific coast of the Americas</li>
<li><i>Selar crumenophthalmus</i>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bigeye" title="Bigeye">bigeye</a> (India)</li>
<li><i>Trachurus capensis</i>, <a href="/wiki/Cape_horse_mackerel" title="Cape horse mackerel">cape horse mackerel</a> (South Africa)</li>
<li><i>Trachurus declivis</i>, <a href="/wiki/Greenback_horse_mackerel" title="Greenback horse mackerel">greenback horse mackerel</a> (Australia)</li>
<li><i>Trachurus japonicus</i>, <a href="/wiki/Japanese_horse_mackerel" title="Japanese horse mackerel">Japanese horse mackerel</a> (Japan)</li>
<li><i>Trachurus novaezelandiae</i>, <a href="/wiki/Yellowtail_horse_mackerel" title="Yellowtail horse mackerel">yellowtail horse mackerel</a> (New Zealand)</li>
<li><i>Trachurus trachurus</i>, <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_horse_mackerel" title="Atlantic horse mackerel">Atlantic horse mackerel</a> (United Kingdom, Ireland)</li>
</ul>
