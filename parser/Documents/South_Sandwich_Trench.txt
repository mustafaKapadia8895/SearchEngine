<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">South Sandwich Trench</h1>

Map of South Sandwich Plate (SAN) shows its position between the <a href="/wiki/Scotia_Plate" title="Scotia Plate">Scotia Plate</a> (SCO), <a href="/wiki/South_American_Plate" title="South American Plate">South American Plate</a> (SAM) and the <a href="/wiki/Antarctic_Plate" title="Antarctic Plate">Antarctic Plate</a> (ANT). There are also visible The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/East_Scotia_Ridge" title="East Scotia Ridge">East Scotia Ridge</a> (ESR), <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/South_Sandwich_Islands" title="South Sandwich Islands">South Sandwich Islands</a> (SSI) and the South Sandwich Trench (SST).</div>
</div>
</div>
<p>The <b>South Sandwich Trench</b> is a deep <a href="/wiki/Volcanic_arc" title="Volcanic arc">arcuate</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ocean_trench" title="Ocean trench">trench</a> in the South Atlantic Ocean lying 100 km to the east of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/South_Sandwich_Islands" title="South Sandwich Islands">South Sandwich Islands</a>. The trench is produced by the <a href="/wiki/Subduction" title="Subduction">subduction</a> of the southernmost portion of the <a href="/wiki/South_American_Plate" title="South American Plate">South American Plate</a> beneath the small <a href="/wiki/South_Sandwich_Plate" title="South Sandwich Plate">South Sandwich Plate</a>. The South Sandwich Islands constitute a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Volcanic" title="Volcanic">volcanic</a> <a href="/wiki/Island_arc" title="Island arc">island arc</a> which results from this active subduction. <a href="/wiki/Mount_Belinda" title="Mount Belinda">Mount Belinda</a> on <a href="/wiki/Montagu_Island" title="Montagu Island">Montagu Island</a> is an active <a href="/wiki/Volcano" title="Volcano">volcano</a>.</p>
<p>It is the deepest <a href="/wiki/Oceanic_trench" title="Oceanic trench">trench</a> of the Southern Atlantic Ocean, and the second deepest of the <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">Atlantic Ocean</a> after the <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico_Trench" title="Puerto Rico Trench">Puerto Rico Trench</a>.</p>
<p>The trench is 965 km long and has a maximum depth of 8,428 meters below sea level. This location, at 55°40'S, 025°55'W, is called <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Meteor_Deep&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Meteor Deep (page does not exist)">Meteor Deep</a>. It is 122 km northeast of <a href="/wiki/Zavodovski_Island" title="Zavodovski Island">Zavodovski Island</a>.</p>
<p>The southernmost portion of the South Sandwich Trench reaches south of 60°S and therefore into the <a href="/wiki/Southern_Ocean" title="Southern Ocean">Southern Ocean</a>, thereby containing the maximum depth of that Ocean at 60°00'S, 024°W, with a depth of 7,235 meters.</p>
