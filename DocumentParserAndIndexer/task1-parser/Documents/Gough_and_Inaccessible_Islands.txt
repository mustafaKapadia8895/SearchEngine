<p><b>Gough and Inaccessible Islands</b> is a <a href="/wiki/UNESCO" title="UNESCO">UNESCO</a> <a href="/wiki/World_Heritage_Site" title="World Heritage Site">World Heritage Site</a> in the archipelago of <a href="/wiki/Tristan_da_Cunha" title="Tristan da Cunha">Tristan da Cunha</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/South_Atlantic_Ocean" title="South Atlantic Ocean">South Atlantic Ocean</a>. It consists of <a href="/wiki/Gough_Island" title="Gough Island">Gough Island</a> <span class="nowrap">(<span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Gough_and_Inaccessible_Islands&amp;params=40.32_S_9.94_W_region:SH_type:isle_dim:50000"><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">40°19′S</span> <span class="longitude">9°56′W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">40.32°S 9.94°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">-40.32; -9.94</span></span></span></a></span>)</span> and <a href="/wiki/Inaccessible_Island" title="Inaccessible Island">Inaccessible Island</a>. <span class="nowrap">(<span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Gough_and_Inaccessible_Islands&amp;params=37.30_S_12.68_W_region:SH_type:isle_dim:30000"><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">37°18′S</span> <span class="longitude">12°41′W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">37.30°S 12.68°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">-37.30; -12.68</span></span></span></a></span>)</span></p>
<p>The islands are uninhabited apart from the personnel of a weather station. They are <a href="/wiki/Important_Bird_Area" title="Important Bird Area">Important Bird Areas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alliance_for_Zero_Extinction" title="Alliance for Zero Extinction">Alliance for Zero Extinction</a> sites.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Spectacled_petrel" title="Spectacled petrel">spectacled petrel</a> is believed to nest only on <a href="/wiki/Inaccessible_Island" title="Inaccessible Island">Inaccessible Island</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<p>Gough Island was inscribed as a World Heritage Site in 1995.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> In 2004 the site was extended. The marine zone of Gough Island was extended from 3 to 12 <a href="/wiki/Nautical_mile" title="Nautical mile">miles</a> and Inaccessible Island was included with the surrounding marine zone extending out to 12 miles. The site was renamed <i>Gough and Inaccessible Islands</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>The selection criteria for the site do not include its geomorphic interest. However, there has been ongoing discussion of including Gough and Inaccessible Islands in a possible "serial trans-boundary nomination" for the <a href="/wiki/Mid-Atlantic_Ridge" title="Mid-Atlantic Ridge">Mid-Atlantic Ridge</a>, which would include other volcanic sites in the Atlantic.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MAR_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-MAR-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
